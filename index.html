Ecosystem of AGENTIC INFRASTRUCTURE 2025

I built this living landscape to make sense of the fast‑moving agentic infrastructure world—for myself and for anyone trying to ship real multi‑agent systems. My goal is to keep a clean, opinionated shortlist of the most up‑to‑date infrastructure, SDKs, models, and techniques that actually help you deliver: from core compute and foundation models, to orchestration frameworks, memory/vector databases, evaluation and observability, tool integrations, and distributed agent map‑reduce. If you’re assembling an agentic stack today, start here. Kick the tires, compare trade‑offs, and choose tools that compound over time.
